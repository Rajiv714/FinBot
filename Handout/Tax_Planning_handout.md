*   **Core Definitions and Terminology with Detailed Explanations:**
    *   **Tax Planning:**  The legal arrangement of financial affairs to minimize tax liabilities.  This involves understanding tax laws, regulations, and available deductions, exemptions, and credits.  It's *not* tax evasion, which is illegal.  Example:  Choosing to invest in a tax-advantaged retirement account is tax planning; hiding income from the IRS is tax evasion.
    *   **Tax Evasion:** Illegally avoiding paying taxes. This involves misrepresenting income, claiming false deductions, or concealing assets.  Tax evasion is a crime and can result in penalties, fines, and imprisonment.
